## STRIDE Threat Register

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|-------------|-----------------|
| T-7-01 | Tampering | HospMetricSchema (new measureKey/source) | mitigate | Add as closed `z.enum` (not free strings) in `view-model.ts` so a crafted POST cannot inject arbitrary grouping keys; the schema is the single validation gate (rule #4, V5). |
